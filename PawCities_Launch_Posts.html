GENEVA
5
🇨🇭
The 4-Hour Loneliness Rule
Swiss law says dogs shouldn't be left alone for more than 4 hours
Did You Know
Switzerland doesn't just love dogs — they legislate it. 🇨🇭 Swiss law says dogs shouldn't be left alone for more than 4 hours. It's one of the strictest loneliness regulations in the world. That's not all: shock collars are banned. All dogs must be microchipped by age 3 months. And owners of dogs over 25kg in Geneva must pass a behavior and training test.
