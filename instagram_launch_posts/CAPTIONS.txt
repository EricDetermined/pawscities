POST 4: Paris Top 3 Facts 🇫🇷
═══════════════════════════════════════════

🇫🇷 3 things you didn't know about dogs in Paris:

1️⃣ There's 1 dog for every 7 Parisians — over 300,000 dogs making Paris one of Europe's most dog-dense cities.

2️⃣ Dogs dine indoors. France has no laws banning dogs from restaurants. Many cafés bring out water bowls!

3️⃣ Don't skip the cleanup — Paris enforces a €68 fine for not picking up after your dog. 💰
